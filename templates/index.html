McD4Me

What is this?

Imagine this: It's 11:00pm on a Wednesday night. You're hanging out in a lounge in your dorm, and then suddenly you get a craving... You could really go for some McDonald's right now. Being the benevolent community member you are, you ask if anyone else wants McDonald's as well, and a flurry of people express their interest. Well now you've got a problem: How are you going to keep track of everyone's order?

Cue McD4Me, a web app to manage large group orders. If you're the one ordering, hit Host below to start an online order room. If you're hopping on an order, hit Join below and enter the room code. The rest should be straightforward.

Note that McD4Me won't do any actual ordering for you, it simply keeps track of all of the orders for a large group.
